Information about this site

Today over 300,000 people are employed by the largest local government in the United States, NYC.

These people work everyday to provide the 8 million residents of the NYC metropolitan area with the best possible service (even though the trains don't run very well) and ensure our safety

The city offers many benefits such as comfortable retirement packages as well as good health care and vactions

City jobs are one of the most important things today. Even in such difficult times, the city still offers all generations with work opportunities

I have been increasingly interested in working for the city because they employ the most high school students in the city (more than any other organization).

When I was searching the web for large data sets, I found a very large API that had every public job opening in the city.

I created this is the site to showcase all of the interesting and facinating statistics that I have graphed about the public work sphere in NYC

Why I chose this topic

I chose this topic because recently I have been more interested in jobs as I was searching for summer work.

Throughout my search public job openings kept on coming up for teens as well as for experienced workers.

Then as I was searching around the internet for large data sets to analyze, I found an API that showed all job openings in NYC which sounded very exciting.

After dwelving into the data, I realized that this could be big opportunity to start learning about data analysis which is a crucial skill in IT.

This topic also seemed the most plausible because the data in the form of a json file was already very neat and only needed some cleaning up.

I also decided to create graphs and chart with that data because I really enjoyed using madplotlibs.
